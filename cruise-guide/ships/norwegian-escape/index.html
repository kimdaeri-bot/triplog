🚢 선박 소개

Norwegian Escape® offers the ultimate playground at sea for all ages. Take hanging out to new heights on the ropes course and dare to walk the plank, which extends eight feet over the side of the ship. Swoosh down waterslides and splash around the Kid’s Aqua Park.

Then slow things down in the Spa Thermal Suite. Dine oceanside along The Waterfront where every table boasts the best views. Roll into good times at the casino, rock out to live music and catch amazing original productions.

And that’s just the beginning. Serve up all that excitement and relaxation with a wide selection of delicious cuisine, from fresh seafood dishes at Bayamo by Ocean Blue to Italian at La Cucina and Le Bistro’s elegant French dishes. Then let the good times flow at one of our unique bars and lounges.

Norwegian Escape is ready to whisk you away to the Bahamas, The Caribbean from Miami and the Mediterranean from Rome. Get ready for the ultimate escape with Norwegian!
